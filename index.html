Description

Two Wizards! A game about remembering things, aiming at things, and shooting at things. So very much like life actually. Two people on the same computer each control a wizard. Some keys are used to move, others to cast and take control of spells mid-flight.

History

This game began as an attempt to create something original. What have I learnt? Being original is hard. Well, the game's still growing.
